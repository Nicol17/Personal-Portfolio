Who am I?

I'm a Front-End Web Developer based in Vancouver, BC. A college student that enjoys developing creative websites and applications. An economist by trade, after working multiple roles in finance, trade and consulting, I transition to tech because I believe that it is going to impact the future in a significant manner and I'd like to put forward my skills and abilities to contribute to that change.
My curiosity fuels me in the pursuit of many areas of study, interests, and hobbies. I’m a fast learner, able to pick up new skills and juggle different projects and roles. I like to develop expertise in a number of areas over the course of my life and career.
